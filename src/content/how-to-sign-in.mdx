# How To Sign In
<Steps>
{<h3>Install Steam</h3>}
Download and install the [Steam desktop app](https://store.steampowered.com/about)

{<h3>Sign In</h3>}
Sign in to your Steam account via the Steam desktop app

<Callout type="info">
    You can sign in to multiple accounts. All accounts will be available on the account selection screen
</Callout>

{<h3>Select Your Account</h3>}
From the `account selection` screen you will see a list of available Steam accounts to choose from. Choose an account to proceed to the `dashboard`
</Steps>
