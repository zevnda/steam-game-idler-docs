# Steam Credentials
 
<Callout type="error">
    Do not share these credentials with anyone
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
    You can skip these steps if you do not plan to use the [Card Farming](/feature/card-farming) feature
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
    Steam accounts with [Family View](https://store.steampowered.com/parental/) _(parental restrictions)_ enabled will have a `steamParental` cookie that needs to be saved in [Settings > Card Farming](/settings/card-farming). In some cases, Steam accounts might have a `steamMachineAuth` cookie that needs to be used as well
</Callout>

<Steps>
### Log In to the Steam Community Website

<Callout type="info">
    You must get your cookies from https://steamcommunity.com/ and not https://store.steam.com/
</Callout>
 
Open your web browser and log in to the official https://steamcommunity.com/ website with the account you plan to farm trading cards on
 
### Find Your Steam Cookies
  
1. Press `F12` to open Developer Tools
2. Navigate to `Application` _(or `Storage`)_
3. Under `Cookies`, select `https://steamcommunity.com/`
4. Locate the `values` for both `sessionid` and `steamLoginSecure`
    * If you have either a `steamParental` and/or `steamMachineAuth` cookie, you will need these `values` as well

<ZoomImage src="/assets/rwQIV1x.png" description="Click to expand" />

### Add Credentials to SGI

1. Open SGI and go to <Icon type="cog" /> [Settings > Card Farming](/settings/card-farming)
2. Paste your credentials into the appropriate fields and click <MockButton type='save' />

### Verify Success

1. Once you click <MockButton type='save' /> you should see a `Card farming as [user_data]` confirmation in the top-right corner
2. If you do not get a success message, confirm your credentials and retry the previous steps

<ZoomImage src="/assets/QB3oJIn.png" description="Click to expand" />

</Steps>
