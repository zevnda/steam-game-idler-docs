# Achievement Manager

The Achievement Manager feature allows you to manually unlock and lock a game's achievements, as well as modify an achievement's statistics.

### Achievements
<Steps>
{<h3>Step 1</h3>}
Click <Icon type='game' /> in the `sidebar`

{<h3>Step 2</h3>}
Hover over the game you wish to view the achievements for, and click <Icon type='award' /> to view to the `achievement manager` screen

{<h3>Step 3</h3>}
On the `achievement manager` screen you will see a list of achievements if any are available for that game

<Callout type="info">
    You can use the search bar at the top of the screen to search for an achievement by its title
</Callout>

{<h3>Step 4</h3>}
Click <MockButton type='unlock' /> or <MockButton type='lock' /> next to an achievement to unlock or lock that specific achievement

Click <MockButton type='unlock-all' /> or <MockButton type='lock-all' /> at the top of the achievements list to unlock or lock all achievements for that game
</Steps>

### Statistics
<Steps>
{<h3>Step 1</h3>}
Click <Icon type='game' /> in the `sidebar`

{<h3>Step 2</h3>}
Hover over the game you wish to view the statistics for, and click <Icon type='award' /> to view to the `achievement manager` screen

{<h3>Step 3</h3>}
On the `achievement manager` screen click the `statistics` tab

{<h3>Step 4</h3>}
Change the `value` of any statistics you want to mody and click <MockButton type='save-changes' />
</Steps>
